Sid Meier's Civilization VI: Rise and Fall

Released: February 8, 2018

The Civilization VI: Rise and Fall expansion launches new Loyalty and Governor systems and tasks players to lead their civilizations through Golden and Dark Ages. Alongside new units, districts, wonders and resources, eight new civilizations and 9 new leaders join the fray.

Rise and Fall is included in both the Platinum Edition and the Anthology Edition

Golden Ages & Dark Ages

As your civilization ebbs and flows, and you reach milestone Historic Moments, you will experience Dark Ages or Golden Ages, each providing specific challenges or bonuses based on your actions in-game. Rise triumphantly from a Dark Age, and your next Golden Age will be even stronger - a Heroic Age.

Loyalty System

Cities now have individual loyalty to your leadership - let it fall too low, and face the consequences of low yields, revolts, and the potential to lose your city to another civilization, or its own independence. But one civilization’s loss can be your gain as you inspire Loyalty among cities throughout the map and further expand your borders.

Governors

Recruit, appoint, and upgrade powerful characters with unique specialization bonuses and promotion trees to customize your cities, and reinforce Loyalty.

Enhanced Alliances

An enhanced alliances system allows players to form different types of alliances and build bonuses over time.

Emergencies

When a civilization grows too powerful, other civilizations can join a pact against the threatening civilization and earn rewards, or penalties, when the Emergency ends.

Timelines

Review your civilization’s history at any time with the new Timeline feature, a visual journey through the Historic Moments that you encountered on your path to victory.

Sid Meier's Civilization VI: Gathering Storm

Released: February 14, 2019

Civilization VI: Gathering Storm introduces an active planet where climate and tectonics present unique new challenges. Floods, droughts, and powerful storms force players to adapt to a changing environment, and the choices players make have a lasting impact on the world's temperature, potentially causing ice caps to melt and sea levels to rise.

Gathering Storm is included in both the Platinum Edition and the Anthology Edition

Environmental Effects

Volcanoes, storms (blizzards, dust storms, tornadoes, hurricanes), climate change, floods, and droughts begin wreaking havoc on the planet.

Power and Consumable Resources

Resources such as coal and oil can now be used to produce power for cities. While initially you'll be powering your most advanced buildings by burning carbon-based resources like coal and oil, but renewable energy sources also unlock as you progress to current-day technologies. Your choices about resource usage will directly affect the world's temperature and can cause melting ice caps and rising sea levels.

New Engineering Projects

Players can now overcome land difficulties with improvements such as canals, dams, tunnels, and railroads. When settling cities, consider the flood risk to coastal lowland areas, but keep in mind that in the late-game, technologies like Flood Barriers can be used to protect these tiles

World Congress and the Diplomatic Victory

Make your voice heard among the other leaders of the world. Earn Diplomatic Favor through Alliances, influencing city-states, competing in World Games, and more. Use Diplomatic Favor to extract promises from other leaders, vote on Resolutions, call a Special Session to address an emergency, and increase the weight of your votes to achieve the new Diplomatic Victory.

A New Era

The technology and civics trees have been expanded with the new Future Era, unlock new improvements such as seasteads, new policies and governments and projects like carbon recapture.

Sid Meier's Civilization VI: New Frontier Pass

Released: 2020-2021

The Civilization VI: New Frontier Pass is a series of six DLC packs released within a year and features another eight new civilizations, nine new leaders, six new game modes, and a variety of new content such as units, wonders, districts, resources and more.

The New Frontier Pass is included in the Anthology Edition

New Game Modes

Six new game modes, provide brand new ways for players to play Civilization VI.

  • Apocalypse Mode: Face increased disasters and the looming threat of a world-ending meteor shower
  • Secret Societies Mode: Join mysterious organizations with powerful bonuses
  • Dramatic Ages Mode: Experience more impactful Golden and Dark Ages
  • Heroes & Legends Mode: Recruit powerful heroes from mythology
  • Monopolies & Corporations Mode: Build economic empires and luxury monopolies
  • Zombies Mode: Survive the undead as fallen units rise again as zombies

New Natural and World Wonders

Discover new natural wonders such as the Bermuda Triangle and construct new world wonders such as Etemenaki and the Biosphère.

New Map and Resources

Unlock the new Highlands and Wetlands maps and new resources to improve; Honey and Maize

New Districts and Buildings

Construct the new Diplomatic Quarter and Preserve districts and their respective buildings: the Consulate & Chancery and the Grove & Sanctuary.

New Leader Personas

Alongside the new leaders, owners of the New Frontier Pass will be given the Teddy Roosevelt and Catherine De Medici persona packs. Each Persona Pack contains a brand new take on each leader, with a new leader model and background, new gameplay bonuses, and an updated agenda that reflect the changes to the leader’s personality.
