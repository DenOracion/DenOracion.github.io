Leaders: Lead a Civilization to the stars

Leaders are representatives of the different civilizations in Civilization VI. The leaders were selected based on historical figures who accomplished great things for their respective nations. Each leader comes with a dedicated unique ability that gives them special bonuses and gameplay advantages in game and a unique agenda that dictates how the AI will play and interact with players.
